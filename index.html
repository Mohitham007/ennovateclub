About Us

The Ennovate Club is a student-centric initiative that promotes collaboration among students from various disciplines. It focuses on integrating skills in events like pitching and technical challenges. By working together on unified projects, members gain practical experience, enhance their self-learning, and improve through real-world problem-solving and teamwork. It mainly focuses on sending students for national tech and pitching events.
